BeachBot: The Robot Cleaner

BeachBot (BB) is our autonomous robot designed to clean beaches efficiently. It uses a camera and AI to detect plastic litter while leaving sand, seaweed, and shells intact.

How BeachBot Works

AI Eyes: Identifies plastic items like bottles and wrappers hidden in the sand.
Gentle Grip: Robotic arms pick up litter without disturbing the environment.
Solar Powered: Uses clean energy to operate for long hours on the beach.

Why It Matters
